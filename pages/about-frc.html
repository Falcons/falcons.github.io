FIRST Robots Competition

Known as “the ultimate sport for the mind,” the FIRST Robotics Competition gives youth hands-on experience with various STEM fields in a diverse and inclusive environment. As a part of the competition, we have about six weeks and a strict budget to build and program a 55 Kg multifunctional robot that meets the stringent requirements of the challenge.
